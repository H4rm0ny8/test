## Why OPSEC matters

Even in labs, building good habits reduces mistakes when you move to real engagements.

## Checklist

- Separate attack infrastructure from personal accounts
- Rotate payloads and C2 profiles per engagement
- Document every command you run (timeline + scope)
- Sanitize screenshots before publishing writeups
